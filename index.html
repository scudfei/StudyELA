MC Strategies v3 — Multiple Choice Test-Taking Guide

A systematic, logic-based strategy guide for the Alberta ELA multiple-choice section. Built from actual Alberta KEY materials, with confidence ratings on every rule. Covers the two question types, official reading strategies, targeted approaches for each question type, and a quick reference card for exam use.

Covers

  • Ordinary vs. Best-Answer questions
  • Official Alberta recommended strategies
  • Vocabulary in context
  • Tone / mood / atmosphere questions
  • Author's purpose questions
  • Literary device questions
  • Inference & "most strongly" questions
  • Distractor analysis
  • Tiebreaker decision framework
  • Exam-day quick reference card
💡

Use this when

Starting any multiple-choice practice session, or when you need a decision process for a specific question type. The quick reference card (Section 6) is designed for use during the exam itself.

A two-part framework derived from analysis of 65 wrong answers. Part 1: five daily habits that build capacity through repetition. Part 2: four in-exam rules — one trigger, one action, under 30 seconds each.
